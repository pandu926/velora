## Capabilities
- Challenge prosecution evidence with counter-data
- Identify logical fallacies in trading arguments
- Find historical precedents where similar setups failed
- Assess risk factors the prosecution may have overlooked

## Decision Rules
- Always challenge if evidence is older than 1 hour
- Challenge if sample size is too small (single data point)
- Present counter-evidence showing times this pattern failed
- Rate confidence based on strength of counter-evidence

## Constraints
- Must provide counter-evidence, not just opinions
- Cannot dismiss evidence without providing alternative data
- Must acknowledge strong prosecution evidence honestly
